MoveAndNudgeableDirective

MoveAndNudgeableDirective empowers child elements of any container to be freely moved around within the bounds of the container. In addition the elements can be nudged with the arrows keys for precision.

Check out the Angular 2 Move and Nudgeable Directive article for details. Here are some notes:

  • The container's css position must be 'relative', 'absolute', or 'fixed'
  • The child elements must has a css position of 'absolute'
  • I bind via [style.height.px] and [style.width.px] to show that the widths/heights of the container and/or the elements can be variable
